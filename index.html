Drop a pin wherever you go.

Days—or even years—later, scroll through your history and think, “Ah, that diner was great,” or “Wait, I bought this coat THERE three years ago?!”

*

Near-by Locations

View a list of places near the map pin and select the one you where you are.

*

Directly from the Map

Spot the place directly on the map with your eagle eyes—just tap it and check in.

*

Manually Check-in

If you can’t find the place on the map (hey, sometimes maps aren’t up to date!), you can manually record it.

*

Bookmarks

If it’s a place you visit often but is hard to find, bookmark it so you can check in quickly next time.

*

Cross-Device Sync

You can back up your data to your calendar to avoid losing your check-ins when you change your device or delete the app.

Your data, your control

The check-in data is all saved on your device. This also means that if you delete the app or change your device, the data will be lost.

The location data is used to get the nearby places.

If you enable calendar syncing, you are agreeing to the calendar provider service's privacy policy.
